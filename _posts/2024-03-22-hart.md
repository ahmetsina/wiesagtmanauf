## Definitions
1. (adjective) Hard, solid, firm
2. (adjective) Rough, tough
3. (adjective) Severe

## Opposites
- Weich (Soft)
- Zart (Delicate)
- Sanft (Gentle)

## Synonyms
- Fest (Solid)
- Steif (Stiff)
- Kompakt (Compact)

## Examples
1. Das Holz war hart und schwer zu bearbeiten. (The wood was hard and difficult to work with.)
2. Er gab mir einen harten Blick. (He gave me a severe look.)
