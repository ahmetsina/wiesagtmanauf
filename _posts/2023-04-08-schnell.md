Schnell is a German word that means "fast" or "quick". It can be used to describe the speed or pace of something or someone. Let's explore some of its definitions, opposites, synonyms and examples:

## Definitions
- schnell: fast, quick

## Opposites
- langsam: slow
- gemächlich: leisurely

## Synonyms
- rasch: quick, rapid
- flink: quick, nimble

## Examples
- Der Zug ist sehr schnell. (The train is very fast.)
- Sie sind sehr schnell gelaufen. (They ran very quickly.)
- Das Auto fährt schneller als das Fahrrad. (The car goes faster than the bike.)
