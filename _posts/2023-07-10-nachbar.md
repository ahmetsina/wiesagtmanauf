**Definition:**

Nachbar (masculine noun) refers to a person who lives close to or next door to someone. It can also be used to describe the person who resides in the neighboring house or apartment. In general, a Nachbar can be anyone residing in close proximity, be it a neighbor in the same building or someone who lives nearby.

**Opposite:**

The opposite of Nachbar can be considered as "Fremder" or "Unbekannter," which means a stranger or unknown person. While a Nachbar is someone with whom you share a living environment, a Fremder is someone you have never met or interacted with before.

**Synonyms:**

- Anwohner - Denoting a person residing in the vicinity or a neighborhood.
- Mitbewohner - Referring to a roommate who shares the same living space.

**Examples:**

1. "Unsere neuen Nachbarn sind sehr freundlich und hilfsbereit." (Our new neighbors are very friendly and helpful.)
   
2. "Der Nachbar hat uns sein Rasenmäher geliehen, damit wir unseren Garten pflegen können." (The neighbor lent us his lawnmower so that we can take care of our garden.)

3. "Ich fühle mich in meiner Nachbarschaft sicher, da meine Nachbarn wachsam sind." (I feel safe in my neighborhood because my neighbors are watchful.)

4. "Die Nachbarskinder spielen oft im gemeinschaftlichen Garten." (The neighbor's children often play in the communal garden.)

5. "Ich rief den Nachbarn um Hilfe, als ich meinen Schlüssel verloren hatte und nicht in meine Wohnung kam."
