## Definitions

- Katze (feminine noun): A domesticated carnivorous mammal, also known as a cat.

## Opposites

- Hund (masculine noun): A domesticated carnivorous mammal, also known as a dog.

## Synonyms

- Mieze (feminine noun): Another colloquial term for a cat.
