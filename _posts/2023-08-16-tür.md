**Definition:** Die Tür is a feminine noun in German which denotes an opening or an entrance in a wall or a structure that allows passage between spaces. It can be made of various materials such as wood, glass, or metal.

**Opposite:** The opposite of Tür is "Wand" (wall), which is a solid structure that separates spaces and blocks access.

**Synonyms:** Some synonyms for Tür include:

1. Eingang (entrance)
2. Zugang (access)
3. Pforte (gate)
4. Tor (gate)

**Examples:**

1. **Bitte schließen Sie die Tür** (Please close the door).
2. **Die Tür ist offen** (The door is open).
3. **Klopf an der Tür** (Knock on the door).
4. **Er stand vor der geöffneten Tür** (He stood in front of the open door).

Türen are essential in providing privacy, security, and enabling movement between areas. They can be found in homes, offices, vehicles, and numerous other structures.
