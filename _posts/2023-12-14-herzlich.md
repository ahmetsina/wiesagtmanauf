## Defintions

- (adjective) Warmhearted; cordial; hearty; sincere in feeling or expression.
- (adverb) Sincerely; cordially.

## Opposites

- Kalt (cold): Unlike "herzlich," this term refers to a lack of warmth or friendliness.

## Synonyms

- Freundlich (friendly): This word is often used interchangeably with "herzlich" to express warm and welcoming behavior.
