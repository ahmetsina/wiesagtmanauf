## Definition
noun

The word "Sonne" in German translates to "sun" in English. It refers to the star at the center of the solar system, which provides light and warmth to Earth.

## Opposite
The opposite of "Sonne" is "Mond," which means "moon" in English. While the sun is associated with daylight and warmth, the moon is associated with nighttime and coolness.

## Synonyms
Some synonyms for "Sonne" include:

- Gestirn (celestial body)
- Himmelskörper (heavenly body)
- Feuerball (fireball)

## Examples
Here are a few examples of how "Sonne" can be used in sentences:

1. Die Sonne scheint heute sehr hell. (The sun is shining very brightly today.)
2. Ich gehe gerne in die Sonne, um mich zu bräunen. (I enjoy going in the sun to get a tan.)
3. Die Blumen benötigen Sonne, um zu wachsen. (The flowers need sun to grow.)
