## Definitions
**vergessen** means "to forget" in English. It is a verb that describes the action of not remembering something or someone.

## Opposites
- **erinnern** - to remember
- **merken** - to notice

## Synonyms
- **vergessen** - vergessen (there is no exact synonym in German)
