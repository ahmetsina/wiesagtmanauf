**Hoffnung** (Hope)

Definition: 

- The feeling of wanting something to happen or be true and thinking that it could happen or be true.
- Optimistic attitude towards the future.

Opposites: 

- Verzweiflung (Desperation)
- Resignation

Synonyms:

- Zuversicht (Confidence)
- Erwartung (Expectation)

Examples: 

1. Ich habe die Hoffnung, dass ich meinen Traumjob bekommen werde. (I have hope that I will get my dream job.)
2. Trotz der aktuellen Schwierigkeiten gibt es immer noch Hoffnung auf eine bessere Zukunft. (Despite the current difficulties, there is still hope for a better future.)
3. Die Hoffnung gibt uns die Kraft, weiterzumachen, auch in schwierigen Zeiten. (Hope gives us the strength to keep going, even in difficult times.)

## Definition
Hoffnung is a German word that translates to "hope" in English. It is derived from the verb "hoffen" which means "to hope". Hoffnung can be defined as a feeling or belief that something positive or desired will happen in the future. It is a fundamental emotion that drives individuals to strive for better outcomes and fuels their optimism.

## Opposites
Antonyms of Hoffnung include Desillusion (disillusion), Verzweiflung (despair), and Resignation (resignation). These words represent the opposite feelings or states of mind, where hope is replaced with feelings of disappointment, hopelessness, and acceptance of a negative outcome.

## Synonyms
There are several synonyms for Hoffnung that are commonly used in German. These include Zuversicht (confidence), Optimismus (optimism), Erwartung (expectation), and Zuversichtlichkeit (positivity). These words capture the essence of hope and reflect a positive mindset towards future events.
