**Morgen** (noun)

Definition:
- The period of time between midnight and noon.
- The early part or beginning of something.

Opposites:
- Abend (evening)
- Nacht (night)

Synonyms:
- Vormittag (forenoon)
- Tagesanbruch (dawn)

Examples:
1. Guten Morgen! - Good morning!
2. Morgen ist ein neuer Tag. - Tomorrow is a new day.
