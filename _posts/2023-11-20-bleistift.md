**Bleistift** (Pencil)

Definition:
A Bleistift is a writing instrument consisting of a narrow cylindrical piece of graphite or colored material encased in a wooden or metal shaft. It is used for writing or drawing on paper or other surfaces.

Synonyms:
- Stift
- Graphitstift

Opposite:
The opposite of a Bleistift would be an **Ausradierstift** (Eraser pencil), which is used to erase or correct mistakes made with a pencil.

Examples:

1. Ich brauche einen Bleistift, um meine Notizen zu machen. (I need a pencil to take my notes.)
2. Kannst du mir bitte deinen Bleistift leihen? (Can you please lend me your pencil?)
