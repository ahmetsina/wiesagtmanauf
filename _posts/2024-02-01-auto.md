## Definition

Das Auto is a German noun that refers to a motor vehicle or an automobile. It encompasses various types of vehicles such as cars, trucks, and motorcycles. 

## Synonyms

Here are some synonyms for "Auto" in the German language:

- Kraftwagen
- Wagen
- Fahrzeug

## Opposite

An antonym for "Auto" in German is "Fahrrad," which means bicycle. While an "Auto" is a motor vehicle powered by an engine, a "Fahrrad" relies on human power to operate.

## Examples

- Ich fahre jeden Tag mit dem Auto zur Arbeit. (I commute to work by car every day.)
- Kannst du mir dein Auto leihen? (Can you lend me your car?)
- Wir sind gestern mit dem Auto in den Urlaub gefahren. (We went on a vacation by car yesterday.)
