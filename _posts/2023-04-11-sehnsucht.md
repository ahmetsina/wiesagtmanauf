Sehnsucht, a German word that can be translated as "longing" or "yearning" in English. It is an intense feeling of desire, often accompanied by a sense of incompleteness or emptiness.

**Definition:** Sehnsucht is an emotional state that arises from a deep desire for something or someone that is unattainable or out of reach. It is often described as a feeling of nostalgia or a yearning for a past or future that may never come.

**Opposites:** The opposite of Sehnsucht can be contentment or satisfaction. While Sehnsucht is a feeling of longing or desire for something unattainable, contentment is a state of being happy and fulfilled with what one has.

**Synonyms:** Sehnsucht can be synonymous with words like desire, craving, yearning, and longing. These words all describe a strong, intense longing for something.

**Examples:**

1. "She felt a deep Sehnsucht for the home she had left behind."
2. "The Sehnsucht for his lost love consumed him."
