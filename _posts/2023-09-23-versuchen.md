## Definition

Versuchen is a German verb that means "to try" or "to attempt." It is derived from the noun "Versuch," which means "attempt" or "experiment." 

## Opposites

- Schaffen: to succeed
- Aufgeben: to give up

## Synonyms

- Probieren: to test
- Erproben: to put to the test
