## Angst

**Definition**:
Fear or anxiety, especially about the future or about something threatening.

**Opposites**:
- Mut (courage)
- Zuversicht (confidence)

**Synonyms**:
- Furcht (dread)
- Sorge (worry)

**Example**:
"Ich habe Angst im Dunkeln." (I am afraid of the dark.)
