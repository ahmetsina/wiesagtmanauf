**Definition:** 

The German word Erdbeere translates to strawberry in English. It is a sweet, juicy, and delicious fruit that is often used in desserts, smoothies, and jams. It is also commonly used in medicinal applications due to its high antioxidant content. 

**Synonyms:** 

- Garden strawberry
- Fragaria ananassa 

**Opposite:** 

The opposite of Erdbeere would be any other fruit that is not a strawberry. An example of such a fruit would be an orange, which is citrusy and sweet but does not have the same characteristic flavor and texture of a strawberry. 

**Examples:** 

- Die Erdbeere schmeckt süß. (The strawberry tastes sweet.)
- Ich esse gerne Erdbeeren im Frühling. (I like to eat strawberries in the spring.)
- Die Erdbeere ist eine wichtige Zutat in vielen Backrezepten. (The strawberry is an important ingredient in many baking recipes.)
