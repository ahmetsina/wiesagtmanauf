## Definitions

1. (noun) The flash of a camera or a camera flash.
2. (noun) A sudden burst of bright light, often associated with thunderstorms.

## Opposites

- Dunkelheit (darkness)
- Schatten (shadow)

## Synonyms

- Scheinwerferlicht (spotlight)
- Strahl (beam)
