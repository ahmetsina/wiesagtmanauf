## Definitions

**Noun:** Licht (das) - light; a source of illumination

## Opposites

- Dunkel - dark
- Schatten - shadow

## Synonyms

- Beleuchtung - illumination
- Helligkeit - brightness

## Examples

1. Die Sonne strahlt helles Licht.
   - The sun shines bright light.
