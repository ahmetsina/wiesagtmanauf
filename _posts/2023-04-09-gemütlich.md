Gemütlich is a German word which means 'cozy' or 'comfortable'. It refers to a warm, inviting and friendly atmosphere. 

**Definitions:** 
- Cozy
- Comfortable 
- Warm 
- Inviting 

**Opposites:** 
- Uncomfortable 
- Cold 
- Hostile 
- Uninviting 

**Synonyms:** 
- Behaglich 
- Angenehm 
- Herzlich 
- Entspannend 

**Examples:** 
- Ein gemütliches Wohnzimmer (A cozy living room)
- Das Bett ist sehr gemütlich (The bed is very comfortable)
- Ein gemütliches Restaurant (A cozy restaurant)
- Wir haben einen gemütlichen Abend zu Hause verbracht (We spent a cozy evening at home) 

Overall, the word gemütlich is often used in German culture to describe a comfortable and relaxed environment.
