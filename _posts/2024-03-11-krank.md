## Krank

**Definition:** 
1. affected by an illness or injury
2. mentally disturbed or distressed

**Opposites:**
1. gesund (healthy)
2. munter (cheerful)

**Synonyms:**
1. unwohl (unwell)
2. krankhaft (pathological)
