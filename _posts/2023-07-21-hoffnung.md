## Definition
"Hoffnung" is a German noun that translates to "hope" in English. It refers to the optimistic feeling or expectancy that something positive will happen or arrive in the future.

## Opposite
The opposite of "Hoffnung" is "Verzweiflung," which means "despair" in English. While hope inspires a positive outlook, despair represents the feeling of complete loss or hopelessness.

## Synonyms
There are several synonyms for "Hoffnung" in the German language, including "Erwartung" (expectation), "Zuversicht" (confidence), and "Optimismus" (optimism).
