## Definitions

- A noun referring to the nocturnal bird known as an owl.
- In colloquial German, "Eule" can also be used to describe someone who is a night owl or stays up late.

## Opposites

- Tag
- Mensch

## Synonyms

- Nachtvogel
- Uhu
