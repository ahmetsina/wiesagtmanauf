## Definition
**Gesundheit** is a German word that translates to "health" in English. It refers to the state of being physically and mentally well, free from illness or injury. This word encompasses the overall well-being of an individual, including their physical, emotional, and social aspects.

## Opposite
The opposite of **Gesundheit** is **Krankheit**, which means "sickness" or "disease" in English. While **Gesundheit** signifies good health, **Krankheit** refers to the presence of an illness or ailment.

## Synonyms
- **Wohlbefinden** (well-being)
- **Fitness** (fitness)
- **Gesundzustand** (state of health)

## Examples
1. Ich fühle mich gut, meine **Gesundheit** ist ausgezeichnet. (I feel good, my health is excellent.)
2. Es ist wichtig, auf seine **Gesundheit** zu achten und regelmäßige Vorsorgeuntersuchungen durchführen zu lassen. (It is important to take care of one's health and undergo regular check-ups.)
3. Ein ausgewogener Lebensstil trägt zur langfristigen **Gesundheit** bei. (A balanced lifestyle contributes to long-term health.)

In conclusion, **Gesundheit** encompasses the holistic well-being of an individual, emphasizing both physical and mental health. Maintaining good health involves making conscious choices and adopting a balanced lifestyle.
