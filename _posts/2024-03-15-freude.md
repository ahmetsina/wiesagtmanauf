## Freude

### Definition:
- pleasure
- joy
- happiness

### Opposites:
- Trauer (sorrow)
- Leid (grief)
- Kummer (sorrow)

### Synonyms:
- Glück (luck)
- Zufriedenheit (contentment) 
- Vergnügen (enjoyment)

### Examples:
1. Die Freude am Lachen (The joy of laughter)
2. Das Glück und die Freude eines Sieges (The happiness and joy of a victory)
