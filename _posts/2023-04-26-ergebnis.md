## Definition

"Ergebnis" is a German word that translates to "result" in English. It refers to the outcome of a process or event that has been executed. It also refers to the solution to a mathematical or scientific problem.

## Synonyms

- Ausgang (outcome)
- Antwort (answer)
- Konsequenz (consequence)
- Resultat (resultant)

## Opposites

- Anfang (beginning)
- Ursache (cause)
- Voraussetzung (prerequisite)

## Examples

- Das Ergebnis des Experiments war unvorhersehbar. (The result of the experiment was unforeseeable.)
- Der Computer hat das Ergebnis der Berechnungen in Sekunden ausgegeben. (The computer generated the result of the calculation in seconds.)
