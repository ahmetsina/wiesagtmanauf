## Definition

Staubsauger is a German word that translates to "vacuum cleaner" in English. It is a compound word formed by combining "Staub" (dust) and "Sauger" (sucker). The literal meaning of Staubsauger is "dust sucker." 

## Opposite

The opposite of Staubsauger would be "Staubmacher," which means "dust maker" or "dust generator." While a vacuum cleaner removes dust and dirt, a Staubmacher would be a device or object that intentionally creates or spreads dust.

1. "Sauger": This is a shortened form of Staubsauger. It is often used informally to refer to a vacuum cleaner.

3. "Saugroboter": This translates to "robotic vacuum cleaner" in English. It refers to an automated device that cleans floors by navigating on its own.
