## Definition

**Frieden** is a German word that means "peace" in English. It is derived from the Old High German word "fridu," which originally meant "freedom."

## Opposites

- **Krieg**: war
- **Gewalt**: violence
- **Hass**: hatred

## Synonyms

- **Ruhe**: calmness
- **Stille**: stillness
- **Harmonie**: harmony
