**Zugang** [noun]

*Definition:*
- The act of entering, accessing, or gaining entry to a place or information.
- The right or permission to enter or use something.

**Opposite:**
- Ausgang (exit)
- Ausschluss (exclusion)

**Synonyms:**
- Eintritt (entry)
- Zutritt (access)
- Eingang (entrance)
- Einstieg (entry point)
